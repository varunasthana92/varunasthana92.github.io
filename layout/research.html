Perception and Robotics Group, University of Maryland, College Park

MorphEyes: Variable Baseline Stereo For Quadrotor Navigation

Morphable design and depth-based visual control are two upcoming trends leading to advancements in the field of quadrotor autonomy. In this paper, a framework for quadrotor navigation is presented based on a stereo camera system whose baseline can be adapted on-the-fly.

G1 continuous bifurcating and multi-bifurcating surface generation with B-splines

Proposed a novel mathematical model using b-splines to construct a bifurcating duct as a single surface, contrary to the conventional approach of stitching various surface patches. Simulated in MATLAB.
